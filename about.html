About Us

IOG is a SAFE Certified company based out of Campbell River and Duncan on Vancouver Island. The company was created to provide clients with full-phase or “ribbon-to-water” resource management and development solutions.

Our speciality is extracting hidden value from development projects while maintaining focus on regulatory requirements and social and environmental responsibilities.

Our Team

OG constructs its project teams based on the unique needs and challenges of each project. Our team consists of Registered Professional Foresters, ISA Certified Arborists, BCFSC Certified Fallers, Qualified Tree Climbers, Danger Tree Assessors and other professionals.

Our Mission

Integrated Operations Group (IOG) is a SAFE Certified company based out of Campbell River and Duncan, British Columbia. Our company was created with the mission to offer full-phase resource management and development solutions to Vancouver Island and beyond.

Our Specialty

We specialize in extracting hidden value from development projects, while maintaining focus on our social and environmental responsibilities as an organization.

IOG considers itself an industry leader in forest management, harvesting/yarding, and tree hazard mitigation and abatement across the forest and resource sectors. In addition to having some of the most experienced fallers, tree climbers and harvesting specialists available on the coast, we employ Registered Professional Foresters, ISA Certified Arborists, Professional Biologists and other resource professionals.
