8 Tips for Using Images in Website Design

Images are a great addition to any site. They help break up the text on your site and give visitors a visual description of the content on the page. Images are a great opportunity to add value to your content and really engage your audience.

  • Make sure your images are relevant to the page's content.
  • Optimize the image file sizes so that they aren't too big.
  • Always include alt attributes and meaningful file names.
  • Never use images to communicate anything that could easily be said with text.
  • Use images to visually break up large areas of text to make it less intimidating.
  • Select photos that look authentic, not like models posing for a stock photo.
  • Try using icons to provide visual cues and explain the content.
  • Always make sure you have the right to use the images legally.

Don’t just use images for the sake of using images. Use images for a greater purpose beyond filling up white space or making a web page more visually interesting. With a little forethought and consideration, images can be a powerful type of content that will enhance the user’s experience on your website.
