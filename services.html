Professional Forestry Services

Snap & Fly Harvesting

Have some timber that you’re unsure of how to access? Have a private parcel that you’d like to harvest, but don’t know where to start? Contact us to learn more about how we can help.

Cut from a foundation of helicopter logging and standing-stem; recovering steep, remote, and constrained timber is our speciality. We can manage each step of the process from feasibility analysis, layout and permitting, right through to harvesting, delivery and sales, along with:

  • Project Management
  • Feasibility analysis
  • Strategic planning
  • Log sales/marketing
  • Planning & Permitting
  • Reconnaissance
  • Forest engineering, layout and field marking
  • Site-plans and permitting
  • GIS, mapping and data-management
  • Cruising and appraisals
  • Harvesting Operations
  • Heli-logging
  • Forestry tree climbing
  • Hand falling
  • Climbing and jigging to support standing-stem operations
  • Conventional ground-based logging
  • Helicopter pad building
  • & more.

Other Services

Maintaining a healthy residual forest is key to any new development. Windfirming and tending to these forest edges can be a key treatment in maintaining aesthetics and more importantly, avoiding windthrow events during storm cycles. In many ways, windfirming is one of the cornerstones of our tree climbing division.
